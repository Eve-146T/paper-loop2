# Paper Loop 2

You are a snek 🐍 and you make your turf bigger and you eat other snek by slicing their tail. 

[<img src="https://fdroid.gitlab.io/artwork/badge/get-it-on.png"
     alt="Get it on F-Droid"
     height="80">](https://f-droid.org/packages/paper.loop2/)

Latest APK: [GitHub releases](https://github.com/Eve-146T/paper-loop2/releases/latest).

## Gameplay

- (You) start on a circle 
- leave the circle and come back without cutting your own tail to expand your territory 
- make sure nobody else cuts your tail otherwise you die 
- opponents are on-device neural bots trained by self-play
